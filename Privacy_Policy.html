Privacy Policy – Discord Music Bot

Last updated: 17 January 2026

1. Data Collection

The bot does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

2. Temporary Processing

Server IDs, voice states, and music queue data are processed in memory only and deleted when no longer needed.

3. Third-Party Services

Audio is streamed from third-party platforms. The bot does not control their privacy policies.

4. Tracking

No cookies, analytics, or tracking mechanisms are used.

5. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

The bot is not intended for users under 13.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated at any time.

8. Contact

Please contact the bot developer via Discord.
